 GraphOmni

Benchmarking Large‑Language‑Models on Graph‑Theoretic Reasoning

Hao Xu*, Xiangru Jian*, Xinjian Zhao, Wei Pang, Chao Zhang et al.  (*equal contribution)
 Paper  Code  Dataset

Key Contributions

  • Comprehensive Coverage: 6 tasks × 3 difficulty tiers × 7 serializations × 9 prompt modes (242 k queries).
  • Realistic Evaluation: Benchmarks 10 leading LLMs (open & closed) and shows representation‑prompt interplay can swing accuracy by 40 pp.
  • RL‑Opt: Lightweight policy‑gradient search finds near‑optimal (prompt, serialization, model) triples with 75 % fewer queries.
  • Extensible Framework: Plug‑and‑play YAML config, HF dataset, batch‑API scripts, and leaderboard.
